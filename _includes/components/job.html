About us

Applied Blockchain is a leading blockchain applications development and consulting company.

Applied Blockchain has been in operation for two years, is self-funded and has built over 15 private blockchain applications, including some that are approaching live production environments.

Applied Blockchain has experience building blockchain solutions for both corporates and startups, including clients in the UK, Switzerland, Australia, China, USA and South America. To date, we have delivered solutions for clients across banking, trade finance, aviation, invoice financing, recruitment, supply chain management and payments. The company has been listed as a Global Top 20 Blockchain Development and Consulting Firm 2017 by Gartner.

Enable trading by a white-listed group of participants in a privacy-preserving environment where trade, volume and price data privacy are protected, even from the venue host. Completed trades can be posted to a blockchain network for post trade and settlement.

The venues acts like a dark pool, useful for secure and confidential off exchange liquidity. Any parties looking to execute trades in an illiquid market may wish to post the trade anonymously, as would in a lit exchange, but they may wish to hide the trade details, volume and price from the open market, in order not to adversely influence the market. Beyond provides an automated solution for matching such trades without revealing the details to the venue host or to an intermediary broker.

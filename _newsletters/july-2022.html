We presented at EthCC5 last week, in the biggest annual Ethereum event in Europe


Last week, Adi Ben-Ari, our CEO, gave a talk at EthCC, in Paris, to a packed room. The talk was entitled “Connecting web2 with web3: Verifying private off chain data sources” and he explained how web3 and DeFi applications can use Silent Data to verify a wallet owner’s web2 accounts including open banking, KYC/AML, social media accounts, while keeping data absolutely private and secure.

Thank you to everyone who came to see us live. If you missed this presentation you can watch the recording below.

EthCC5 wrap up with great talks from Vitalik, Eli Ben-Sasson and Sajida Zouarhi


EthCC was a great way to stay up to date with what the future holds for the Ethereum community. Vitalik gave his thoughts on what will be the future of the Ethereum protocol but we've seen two other very interesting talks.

One from Eli Ben-Sasson, Co-Founder of StarkWare, that shared his vision for the future of this permissionless decentralized ZK-Rollup that operates as an L2 network over Ethereum, as well the new challenges that come ahead.

The other from Sajida Zouarhi, Blockchain Architect, that focused on Ethereum's transition from proof-of-work to proof-of-stake and gave an overview of the Merge architecture.

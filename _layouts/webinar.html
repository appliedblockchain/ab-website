Webinar podcast

Applied Blockchain is pleased to present an online session aimed at a business audience to learn about the emerging technology of zero knowledge proofs and how it can be applied to enhance security and privacy in a business setting.

We examine a range of applications, from identity and personal data privacy, through to regulatory compliance in financial services. The technology can be used standalone in a data / analytics environment, or with a blockchain.
